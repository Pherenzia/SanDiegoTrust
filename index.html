Our Services

Comprehensive support for your study abroad journey

Finding Your Home

With rising rents and stricter rental conditions, it has become challenging for Japanese residents to find housing on their own in San Diego. We'll support you every step of the way, using our trusted local network to help you find the perfect homestay or room rental that fits your needs.

Community College

Some community colleges accept English proficiency scores such as Eiken or Duolingo, making admission easier compared to four-year universities. Tuition is also significantly more affordable. One of the main advantages is that students can improve their English skills while taking courses in their field of interest. After completing two years of study, it's possible to transfer to a four-year university, such as the University of California or California State University. From school selection and application procedures to local support after arrival, we provide comprehensive assistance every step of the way.

Language Schools

From university-affiliated language programs to well-known international schools recognized in Japan, as well as affordable and friendly local schools that can only be found here in San Diego, we offer a wide range of options. Based on your goals and preferences, we'll recommend the school that best fits your needs and support you throughout the entire enrollment process.

Student Visa Application Support

If you plan to study in the U.S. for more than 90 days, you'll need to obtain an F-1 student visa. We handle all the complicated steps for you‚Äîfrom receiving your school's admission document (Form I-20) and paying the SEVIS fee, to completing the DS-160 visa application form and scheduling your embassy interview. Our goal is to make sure even first-time applicants can prepare for studying abroad with confidence, through careful and reliable support every step of the way.

Kids' Camps

We offer a wide variety of camps during spring, summer, and winter breaks, where children can not only learn English but also experience exciting programs in sports, art, science, and more. We'll help you choose the best camp based on your child's age and interests, and provide full support from registration to participation. Even if it's their first time abroad, you can feel confident knowing we provide attentive, local support to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

Consultation & Support

We're available for questions or consultations not only by email, but also through video calls on Zoom or LINE. If any issues or troubles arise during your stay, our responsive support system allows us to assist you quickly and reliably, helping ease your concerns and provide peace of mind every step of the way.

Our Story

It all began when I watched the movie Top Gun as a student. The bright California skies, the open beaches, and the vibrant people on screen captured my heart and eventually led me to San Diego.

I've lived in the United States for 26 years, and in San Diego for 20 of those. With its mild weather all year round, friendly people, and easy access to Japanese food and restaurants, San Diego is truly a wonderful and comfortable place to live.

I started San Diego Trust with a wish to share the beauty of this amazing city with more people and to help those who come here live with peace of mind.

Our mission is to provide thoughtful, reliable support so you can always feel at ease. Just like being with family.
