Our Services

Comprehensive support for your study abroad journey

University Placement

San Diego is home to many community colleges. We will help you find the school that best fits your goals and preferences, and guide you through the entire application process with care and precision.

Housing Assistance

In recent years, rent prices have risen and rental conditions have become more demanding in San Diego. It can be quite difficult for Japanese students to rent an apartment on their own. Using our local network, we provide thorough support in finding housing that suits your needs.

Visa Support

Visa procedures often involve extensive English communication and documentation, which can be overwhelming. We offer complete guidance on the visa application process—from preparing the necessary documents to submission—so you can move forward with peace of mind.

Language Support

From well-known, established language schools to more affordable and welcoming local schools that are only available through local contacts, we offer a variety of options. We will introduce you to the school that best suits your goals and help you complete all necessary enrollment procedures

Cultural Integration

In addition to English learning, there are many summer camps offering diverse programs such as sports, art, and science. We will help you find the ideal camp based on your child's interests and age, and support you from the application stage through to participation. Even for a first-time experience abroad, our local, attentive support ensures you can join with confidence

24/7 Support

We’re always available to answer your questions and concerns—whether by email, video call via Zoom or LINE, or by phone. Our dependable support system allows you to reach out anytime, helping to ease your worries and provide clear answers quickly.

About San Diego Trust

We offer the best plans tailored to meet your needs. Our mission is to help exchange students and expatriate families from Japan live comfortably and safely in San Diego. From choosing the right school to finding housing and providing day-to-day support, we offer comprehensive assistance to ensure a smooth transition. With years of experience and a strong local network, we deliver personalized and attentive support to each individual.

Our team of experienced professionals understands the unique challenges international students face and provides personalized support every step of the way.
